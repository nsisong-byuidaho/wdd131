HaQtec Solutions - Website Plan

Site Name

HaQtec Solutions

The name "HaQtec Solutions" reflects the company's focus on **IT consulting, technology solutions, project management and innovation**. It represents a commitment to providing cutting-edge IT services.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase **HaQtec Solutions' IT services** such as cloud computing, cybersecurity, AI automation, and IT consulting. The site will provide potential clients with information about our services, company values, and ways to contact us.

Scenarios

  • How can HaQtec Solutions help my business improve its cybersecurity?
  • What are the benefits of AI and automation for small businesses?
  • How do I contact HaQtec Solutions for a consultation?

Color Schema

The website will use the following colors:

  • #003366 (Dark Blue) - Used for headings, navigation bar, primary elements and footer.
  • #00A8E8 (Bright Blue) - Used for accents, buttons, and highlights.
  • #F4F4F4 (Light Gray) - Used for background elements to ensure readability.

Typography

The website will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto - Used for headings and navigation items.
  • Roboto - Used for body text and paragraphs.
